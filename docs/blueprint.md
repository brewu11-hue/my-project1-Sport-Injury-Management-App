# **App Name**: Injury Insights

## Core Features:

- Injury Risk Assessment: AI-powered tool to analyze athlete profiles and predict potential injury risks based on training load, past injuries, and other relevant factors.
- Personalized Prevention Plans: Generate custom prevention plans based on individual risk assessments, including specific exercises, stretches, and recovery protocols.
- Injury Tracking: Log and track sport injuries, detailing the type, severity, and date of the injury.
- Treatment and Recovery Log: Log specific treatments and activities used in the recovery phase
- Progress Visualization: Chart injury recovery and improvements to illustrate rehabilitation efforts and achievements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2962FF) to convey trust and professionalism, vital for health-related applications.
- Background color: Light blue (#E6EDFF), a desaturated version of the primary hue, providing a calm and clean backdrop.
- Accent color: Purple (#9D29FF), analogous to the primary hue, adding contrast and signaling interactive elements.
- Font: 'Inter' sans-serif for clear and modern readability across all devices.
- Use clear, universally recognized icons to represent different injury types, recovery stages, and management actions.
- Implement a clean, intuitive layout with easy-to-navigate sections for logging, tracking, and viewing recovery progress.
- Employ subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without distracting from the core functionality.
